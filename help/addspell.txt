Adds a spell.

addspell [spellname] [spellmessage] [spellpoints] [spelldamage] [spelllevel] [spellclass]

spellname 	- name of the spell
spellmessage 	- message displayed when the spell is used
spellpoints 	- number of magic points used by the spell
spelldamage	- number of points of daage done by the spell
spelllevel	- user level for the spell
spellclass	- class of player
