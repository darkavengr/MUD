
There are a number of commands for modifying various characteristics of your player
er'.  Help on them is available in the
 following topics:

@describe me [description]  	  -- setting what others see when they look at you
@gender [male | female | neutral] -- changing your player's gender
@password [password] 		  -- changing your player's password
@sethome [room]			  -- changing your designated home room
inv				  -- displays inventory
score				  -- displays your score
