Sets user level (wizard only)

setrace [level]

Where [level] can be any of the following levels:
novice 	 	novice
warrior		warrior
hero		heroine
champion	championess
superhero	superheroine
enchanter	enchanteress
sorceror	sorceress
necromancer	necromanceress
legend		legend
wizard		witch
Arch Wizard	arch witch
Dungeon Master	dungeon misteress

or [level] can be a number from 1-12
or +[level] to add a level
or -[level]t to subtract levels
